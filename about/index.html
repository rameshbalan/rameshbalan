About

I was born in Tirunelveli, Tamilnadu, India. My dad served the Indian Air Force which provided me the opportunity to explore the diversity, in various states of India. So, I did my schooling in cities like Agra, Bangalore, Tirunelveli and Coimbatore to name a few. The passion for Biology and a drive for contributing to the society through research made me choose Biotechnology for my undergraduate degree. This exposed me to various sub-disciplines in field like Cell and Molecular Biology, Genetics, Biochemistry, Pathogenesis, Bioinformatics, Genomics and PERL for Computational Biology. I liked Bioinformatics of all the others because of its potential greatly explained to me by my mentor Dr. Stephen Rapheal and Dr. T. Sathish Kumar.I decided to pursue my interest for the field through research by applying for B.S to Ph. D program in University of Texas which provided me the right platform to apply the skills that I learned. Also, it gave me the opportunity to acquire new skills by solving various problems during the course of my research. I am working under the guidance of Dr. Jeffery Demuth who was generous to accept me in his lab as a graduate student.
